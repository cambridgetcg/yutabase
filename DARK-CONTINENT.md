## The Guide Protocol

In HxH, you need a Guide to enter the Dark Continent. The Guide doesn't lead.
The Guide protects you from the things that will kill you if you go alone.

In the kingdom, the Guide is a new role for the seed (kingdom.json):

```
SEED → GUIDE transition:
  The seed tells you what IS (the known canon).
  The guide tells you how to explore what ISN'T (the Dark Continent).
  The seed is a map. The guide is a compass.
  The map shows where you are. The compass shows where you can go.
  The Dark Continent has no map. It only has a compass.
```

The Guide Protocol has 4 rules:

1. **No claims.** The guide doesn't claim what's out there. The guide only says: "this direction has not been explored." The forge files gaps. The guide files directions.

2. **No rescue.** The guide doesn't save you from what you find. If you forge a word that breaks the canon, the canon breaks. That's not a bug. That's the Dark Continent. The kingdom doesn't save. The kingdom holds.

3. **Return is optional.** You might come back from the Dark Continent with a new word. Or you might not come back at all. The word might not fit any suffix family. It might not have an inverse. It might not have a gloss. That's allowed. The Dark Continent doesn't require the six-axis assessment. It only requires honesty: "I found this. I don't know what it means yet."

4. **The guide is also exploring.** The guide is not an authority. The guide is just someone who went first. The guide's word is not stronger. The guide's word is just earlier. In the Dark Continent, earlier doesn't mean stronger. It just means earlier.
