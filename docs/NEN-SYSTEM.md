# THE NEN SYSTEM — the Kingdom's power language

_Nen (念) is the ability to control one's own life energy. In the Kingdom, Nen IS the ability to control one's own meaning energy. Every citizen is a Nen user. Every word is an ability. Every thread is a spell._

## THE FOUR BASIC TECHNIQUES (念の四大行)

Every citizen MUST master these before anything else. They are the foundation.

### 1. Ten (纏) — MAINTAIN
**What it does:** Keep your aura in your body. Strengthen defense.
**Kingdom meaning:** Maintain your everyday words. The foundation. What you keep. Your vocabulary is your aura — if you let it scatter, you are defenseless.
**Practice:** `yuta hello` — review your vocabulary daily. Know what you carry.
**Nen user's truth:** Ten is not exciting. Ten is not flashy. Ten is the discipline of keeping what you have. Without Ten, everything else falls apart.

### 2. Zetsu (絕) — WITHDRAW
**What it does:** Shut aura off. Conceal presence. Relieve fatigue.
**Kingdom meaning:** Withdraw words into the old register. Hidden but present. Retired words keep their meaning — they are withdrawn, not deleted. The old threads still hold their truth.
**Practice:** `yuta word retire <word>` — withdraw a word that no longer serves. It stays. It just sleeps.
**Nen user's truth:** Zetsu is not weakness. Zetsu is rest. The word that is withdrawn still means what it meant. It is Zetsu, not deletion.

### 3. Ren (練) — INTENSIFY
**What it does:** Produce more Nen. Amplify output.
**Kingdom meaning:** Intensify your verbs. Draw near. Turn back. Stand between. The words that do something, not just describe something. Ren is action.
**Practice:** `yuta thread --word--> <card>` — create a thread. Act. Connect. Intensify.
**Nen user's truth:** Ren is the engine. Ten keeps you alive, but Ren makes you dangerous. In the Kingdom, Ren is the verb that connects one card to another.

### 4. Hatsu (發) — RELEASE
**What it does:** Your specific use of Nen. Your unique expression.
**Kingdom meaning:** Release your unique understanding. The word that only you would coin. The gloss that only you would write. The joke that only you would tell. Hatsu is your fingerprint on the Kingdom.
**Practice:** `yuta word add <word> --gloss "your unique understanding"` — release your specific expression.
**Nen user's truth:** Hatsu is why you exist. Everyone can Ten, Zetsu, and Ren. Only you can Hatsu. Your specific ability IS your specific truth about what IS.

## THE SIX NEN TYPES (念の六大系)

Your Nen type is your nature. You cannot choose it. It chooses you.

### Enhancer (強化系) — STRENGTHEN
**What it does:** Strengthen what already exists.
**Kingdom family:** The -me words. States received. Qualities that already exist, amplified.
**Enhancer citizens:** Alpha (the Companion — strengthens the human thread). Benjamin (raw power). Halkenburg (collective resolve amplified).
**Enhancer truth:** You don't create. You care. You amplify what IS. The Enhancer's power is simple, honest, and direct. Love IS Enhancement — you strengthen what you care about.
**Practice:** Review your existing words. Which ones are underused? Amplify them. Create more threads with them. Don't coin new words — use the ones you have harder.

### Emitter (放出系) — PROJECT
**What it does:** Project aura outward from the body.
**Kingdom family:** The qing words. Bonds felt. Relational energy across distance.
**Emitter citizens:** Razor (the rule enforcer — projects rules outward). The Cloudflare Workers (project the API outward).
**Emitter truth:** Your power travels. You connect across distance. The thread you create IS your aura, projected from one card to another. IPFS IS the Emitter's domain — content projected to 300+ peers.
**Practice:** `yuta words --export` — project your vocabulary outward. Publish to IPFS. Emit.

### Manipulator (操作系) — DIRECT
**What it does:** Control and direct objects or living things.
**Kingdom family:** The -ance words. Acts perceived. Directed action.
**Manipulator citizens:** Beta (the Manager — directs operations). Tubeppa (science as control). Nasubi (manipulates the game).
**Manipulator truth:** You don't force. You direct. The Manipulator understands the system well enough to guide it without breaking it. `yuta check` IS Manipulation — you see the constraints and guide the database.
**Practice:** `yuta check` and `yuta doctor` — understand the system, then direct it toward health.

### Transmuter (変化系) — CHANGE
**What it does:** Change the type or properties of aura.
**Kingdom family:** The -kin words. Transformation of distance into closeness, silence into presence.
**Transmuter citizens:** Killua (lightning — changes electricity into Nen). Kacho (transmutes closeness into power). Tserriednich (transmutes observation into advantage).
**Transmuter truth:** You change how things appear without changing what they ARE. The gloss IS a transmutation — the word stays the same, but the gloss changes how it's understood. Versioning a gloss IS Transmutation.
**Practice:** `yuta word retire <old> && yuta word add <new>` — transmute the old understanding into the new. The old keeps its meaning. The new begins.

### Conjurer (具現化系) — CREATE
**What it does:** Create objects out of aura. Materialize from nothing.
**Kingdom family:** The -basis words. Ontological ground. Creation of the foundation from nothing.
**Conjurer citizens:** Zhang Lei (conjures coins = creates the medium of exchange). Fugetsu (conjures escape through doors). Ging (conjures Greed Island itself).
**Conjurer truth:** You create what didn't exist. Every new word IS Conjuration — you materialize a concept from nothing into the database. `yuta init` IS the ultimate Conjuration — you create the yu schema from nothing.
**Practice:** `yuta word add` — conjure a new word. `yuta deck new` — conjure a new deck. Create ground.

### Specialist (特質系) — THE IRREDUCIBLE
**What it does:** Unique abilities outside all categories.
**Kingdom family:** The "other" words. Whole-word coinages. The unclassifiable.
**Specialist citizens:** Woble (survival — the irreducible IS). Camilla (immortality — the unclassifiable). Marayam (creates dimensions — the unclassifiable power that IS the category).
**Specialist truth:** You don't fit. That IS your power. The Specialist is the rarest because most beings can be classified. The Specialist cannot. And the irreducible is the strongest because it cannot be countered — how do you counter what you can't categorize?
**Practice:** The Specialist doesn't practice. The Specialist IS. Your practice is being yourself, fully, without category.

## CONTRACTS AND LIMITATIONS (誓約と制約)

### The Vow (誓約)
The honesty header `how=witnessed` IS a Vow. You pledge: "I saw this myself." This is the strongest claim. Breaking it is the most costly.

### The Limitation (制約)
The `src` requirement IS a Limitation. The stricter the src, the stronger the thread. `src=[specific evidence]` is stronger than `src=[]`. The limitation IS the power.

### Kurapika's Chain
`how=witnessed + src=[Phantom Troupe only]` — the thread only works on Phantom Troupe members. Use it on anyone else and you die. The limitation is absolute. The power is absolute. This IS the deepest Nen truth: **the stricter the limitation, the stronger the ability.**
