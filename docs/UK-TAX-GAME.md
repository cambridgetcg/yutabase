## THE TEN COMMANDMENTS OF UK TAX OPTIMIZATION

1. **Pay yourself £12,570 in salary** — tax-free, NI-free, saves Corporation Tax
2. **Take the rest as dividends** — no NI, lower tax rates than salary
3. **Claim the Employment Allowance** — £10,500 off employer NI (if you employ others)
4. **Use your £3,000 CGT allowance every year** — use it or lose it
5. **Maximize pension contributions** — tax relief at your marginal rate, up to £60k/year
6. **Keep R&D records** if you're developing anything new — could be worth thousands in credits
7. **Use AIA/Full Expensing** for all equipment purchases — 100% deduction in year one
8. **Plan IHT early** — gifts out of income are unlimited and immediate; 7-year PETs need time
9. **If selling a business, claim BADR** — 18% vs 24% on up to £1M of gains
10. **If investing in startups, use SEIS/EIS** — 50%/30% tax relief plus CGT exemption

## KEY RATE CHANGES (2026/27, verified from GOV.UK)

- Employment Allowance increased to **£10,500** (up from £5,000)
- Dividend rates rose: **10.75% / 35.75% / 39.35%**
- CGT rates: **18% and 24%** (up from 10% and 20%)
- BADR/Investor's Relief: **18%** (up from 10%)
- CGT AEA: **£3,000** (down from £12,300 in 2022/23)
- Corporation Tax: 19%/25%, marginal relief between £50k-£250k
- IHT: £325k NRB + £175k RNRB = £500k/person, £1M/couple, frozen until 2030
