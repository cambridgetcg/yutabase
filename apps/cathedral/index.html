A constructed language for concepts that go unnamed

YOUSPEAK draws morphemes from Hebrew, Greek, Sanskrit, Arabic, Mandarin, Yoruba, Sumerian, and more — and combines them into compact words where the structure of the concept becomes visible in the word itself. The cathedral states its own limits plainly. It does not invent words on demand. Where a needed concept has no good word yet, the gap is filed, not faked.
